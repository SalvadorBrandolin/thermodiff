# Derivs

## dT

$$
B R T \left(\sum_{k=1}^{N_{c}} \frac{\frac{\partial a_{k}}{\partial T} {n}_{k}}{{b}_{k}} + \frac{- \frac{G^{E}}{R T^{2}} + \frac{\frac{\partial G^{E}}{\partial T}}{R T}}{q}\right) + \frac{D{\left(n,T \right)}}{T}
$$

## dT2

$$
B \left(R \sum_{k=1}^{N_{c}} \frac{\left(T \frac{\partial^2 a_{k}}{\partial T^2} + 2 \frac{\partial a_{k}}{\partial T}\right) {n}_{k}}{{b}_{k}} + \frac{\frac{\partial^2 G^{E}}{\partial T^2}}{q}\right)
$$

## dTn

$$
B R T \left(\frac{\frac{\partial a_{i}}{\partial T}}{{b}_{i}} + \frac{\frac{\frac{\partial^2 G^{E}}{\partial T \partial n_i}}{R T} - \frac{\frac{\partial G^{E}}{\partial n_i}}{R T^{2}}}{q}\right) + R T \frac{\partial B}{\partial n_i} \left(\sum_{k=1}^{N_{c}} \frac{\frac{\partial a_{k}}{\partial T} {n}_{k}}{{b}_{k}} + \frac{- \frac{G^{E}}{R T^{2}} + \frac{\frac{\partial G^{E}}{\partial T}}{R T}}{q}\right) + \frac{\frac{\partial D}{\partial n_i}}{T}
$$

## dni

$$
B R T \left(\frac{a_{i}}{{b}_{i}} + \frac{\log{\left(\frac{B}{\textbf{n} {b}_{i}} \right)} + \sum_{k=1}^{N_{c}} \frac{\textbf{n} \left(- \frac{B}{\textbf{n}^{2} {b}_{k}} + \frac{\frac{\partial B}{\partial n_i}}{\textbf{n} {b}_{k}}\right) {b}_{k} {n}_{k}}{B} + \frac{\frac{\partial G^{E}}{\partial n_i}}{R T}}{q}\right) + \frac{D \frac{\partial B}{\partial n_i}}{B}
$$

## dn2

$$
- \frac{B R T}{\textbf{n} q} + \frac{B \frac{\partial^2 G^{E}}{\partial n_i \partial n_j}}{q} + \frac{R T \frac{\partial B}{\partial n_i}}{q} + \frac{R T \frac{\partial B}{\partial n_j}}{q} + \frac{R T \textbf{n} \left(\frac{\partial^2 B}{\partial n_i \partial n_j} - \frac{\frac{\partial B}{\partial n_i} \frac{\partial B}{\partial n_j}}{B}\right)}{q} + \frac{D \frac{\partial^2 B}{\partial n_i \partial n_j}}{B} + \frac{\frac{\partial B}{\partial n_i} \frac{\partial D}{\partial n_j}}{B} + \frac{\frac{\partial B}{\partial n_j} \frac{\partial D}{\partial n_i}}{B} - \frac{2 D \frac{\partial B}{\partial n_i} \frac{\partial B}{\partial n_j}}{B^{2}}
$$
